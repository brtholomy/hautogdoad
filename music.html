Survivalist Spirals

Spring 2021

"The fiery gate, disillusionment. The power of a question mark, hanging all over what you love."

From the overpowering hypocrisies of COVID, to the rediscovery and refinement of inner strength in the wake of many losses, to the joys of deepening solitude: this album traces a path many of you are already familiar with.


Increscunt Animi

Fall 2021

My voice grew confident. All concerns for innovating in the purely technical aspects of music seem like a deadend. What matters is that my words get across, that my voice is strong and precise in its emotional coloration, that the imagination soars, the mind reels, the heart swells.

That "rap" - which is chant, song, the urform of verse and chorus, the power of an inspired human voice - is what we need. That we are seeking the medicine once handed out by elders around the fire. The old songs, the real songs, the deep knowing. Ceremony is lacking, and yet still alongside us every day waiting to be rediscovered: even if it's in the guise of the "pop" song, we must work our way back to it...
